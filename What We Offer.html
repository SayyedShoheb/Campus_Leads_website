Pre-Incubation Program



India's first Pre Incubation programme brought you by Edventure Park! :)
The Pre-incubation Program is a 4 month long program which focusses on making the idea a realistic solution.
It comprises of tasks where the pre incubatees have to figure out their Vision and Mission statement,
Validate their ideas, build Prototypes, get their hands dirty in different aspects/fields
and get an MVP (Minimum Viable Product) ready.
